## Principles

1.	**Exploration:** \
We recognize the importance of space exploration for the future of humanity, both in terms of scientific discovery and potential resources.
Therefore, we will actively explore and chart the vast expanse of space, seeking out new worlds and discovering the mysteries of the universe.

2.	**Cooperation:** \
We will work together with all nations and organizations to advance our understanding of space and to achieve common goals.
We prioritize international cooperation in space endeavors, in order to share knowledge and to prevent conflicts.
We will work together as a global community, pooling our resources and knowledge to achieve common goals.

3.	**Sustainability:** \
We continuously monitor and evaluate the social and environmental impacts of space activities.
We invest in the development of sustainable and cost-effective technology for space travel and colonization.
We will use sustainable and environmentally friendly technologies to minimize our impact on the cosmos.
We will prioritize sustainable practices in all of our space-faring endeavors, ensuring that future generations will have the opportunity to continue exploring and learning from the cosmos.
We will take steps to protect and preserve the resources and environments of other celestial bodies, recognizing that they may have unique scientific, historical, or cultural value.

4.	**Innovation:** \
We will push the boundaries of science and technology, developing new and revolutionary ways to travel, live, and thrive in space.
We will embrace new technologies and approaches to push augment the possibilities in space exploration and development.

5.	**Education:** \
We promote education and outreach programs to inspire the next generation of space explorers and scientists.
We will educate future generations about the wonders of space and inspire them to take part in humanity's journey into the stars.
We encourage public participation in space exploration by creating opportunities for citizen scientists, space enthusiasts, and other members of the public to contribute to space-related research and activities.
We will make knowledge and information about space accessible to all, encouraging a new generation of explorers and scientists.

6.	**Peace:** \
We will use our shared passion for space exploration to bring people together and promote peace and understanding between nations and cultures.
We respect and protect the rights of all space-faring nations and individuals, and work to prevent the weaponization of space.
We will use space for peaceful purposes and avoid weaponizing space.

7.	**Survival:** \
We will ensure the survival and prosperity of our species by establishing permanent settlements on other planets, as a hedge against the eventual extinction of humanity on Earth.
We work towards the long-term goal of making humanity a multi-planetary species, in order to ensure the survival of our species in the event of a global catastrophe.
We recognize the potential risks and challenges associated with space exploration, such as space debris and the impact on celestial bodies, and take steps to mitigate them.

8.	**Ethics:** \
We will respect the potential for other forms of intelligent life, and consider the ethical implications of our actions in space.
We will develop and utilize space resources in a responsible and ethical manner, taking into account the potential impact on both Earth and space environments.

9.	**Openness:** \
We Foster a culture of openness and transparency in space activities, in order to build trust and understanding among nations and peoples.
We will promote open access to the data and knowledge gained through space exploration and encourage the participation of people from all backgrounds.
We will strive to make space travel and exploration accessible to all, regardless of background or economic status.

10.	**Progress:** \
We will strive to make steady progress in our understanding and mastery of space, while being mindful of the potential risks and benefits of our actions.
We will plan for and invest in long-term space exploration and colonization efforts that will benefit humanity for generations to come.
